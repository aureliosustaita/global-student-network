The Global Student Network at UCLA is a dynamic student organization dedicated to fostering international collaboration and cultural exchange. With a growing network of diverse members, the organization connects students from various backgrounds, providing opportunities for leadership, networking, and professional development. Founded to support global engagement, the Global Student Network hosts events, workshops, and initiatives that bridge communities across campus and beyond. Based at the University of California, Los Angeles, the organization continues to expand its reach, promoting inclusivity and cross-cultural dialogue among students from all disciplines.
